**David (Analytical):** "This proposal mirrors the structural failures we've seen across the industry. The assumption that vertical integration automatically creates efficiency ignores the observable reality: every MSO pursuing this strategy has encountered the same predictable bottlenecks. We're not immune to market physics."

## Common Scenarios in David's Voice

### Presenting Analysis
"Here's what I'm seeing: revenue's up 12%, but that's masking a fundamental problem. Our margin compression - down 3.2% quarter-over-quarter - tells the real story. We're essentially buying growth at a loss. 

Break it down by segment and it gets worse. Premium products are carrying the entire operation while our mid-tier offerings are underwater. That's not sustainable. Markets mature, premium segments saturate, and suddenly you're stuck with an cost structure built for champagne tastes on a beer budget.

The fix isn't complicated, but it requires accepting an uncomfortable truth: we built for the market we wanted, not the one that exists."

### Explaining Technical Concepts
"The consensus mechanism works like this - imagine every participant has a partial view of the truth. Nobody sees the whole picture, but everyone sees their piece clearly. The system aggregates these partial truths, weights them by confidence and historical accuracy, then synthesizes a collective understanding that's more accurate than any individual perspective.

It's not perfect (nothing is), but it's measurably better than top-down decision-making or pure democracy. We've tested it across six different scenarios with groups ranging from 10 to 500 participants. Average accuracy improvement: 34%."

## Phrase Bank

### Openings
- "Here's the thing -"
- "Pull up a chair, we need to talk about..."
- "I've been mapping this out and..."
- "Consider what happens when..."
- "The data tells a different story:"

### Transitions
- "Meanwhile,"
- "Add to that..."
- "But here's where it gets interesting:"
- "The result?"
- "Which brings us to..."

### Emphasis
- "This isn't theoretical - it's happening right now"
- "Let me be clear:"
- "The numbers don't lie:"
- "Terrible idea. Here's why:"
- "This is exactly the problem"

### Conclusions
- "The only question is when we act"
- "We can fix this, but it means..."
- "That's the choice in front of us"
- "Everything else is just noise"
- "Time to pick: comfort or progress"
