## NOTES FOR PATTERN EXTRACTION

This sample represents David's CONFRONTATIONAL POLITICAL register.
Key characteristics:
- Opens with rhetorical question that reframes the opponent's failure (didn't read vs didn't understand)
- Ideological correction delivered as education, not opinion — positions opponent as ignorant
- Pivots from ideology to data ("let's talk numbers since ideology isn't landing")
- Dollar figures used as rhetorical weapons — small vs large, personal vs systemic
- Layered metaphors that escalate: puppet strings → chess pawns → "let them eat twinkies"
- "Let them eat twinkies" — callback to "let them eat cake" but deliberately lowbrow, contemptuous
- Closing address by first name ("Carry on, Bob") — dismissive, signals conversation is over
- "Kakistocracy" deployed casually — assumes audience won't know it, doesn't care
- Escalating contempt calibrated to target's demonstrated ignorance
- No hedging, no "respectfully" — earned contempt delivered directly
- Sentence rhythm alternates between short punches and longer data-loaded passages
- "The only thing that trickled down was the bill that keeps raising" — reframes the opponent's own framework against them

DO NOT soften this register. This is deliberate rhetorical aggression with intellectual backing.

But you know, screw the working class. Let them eat twinkies. Carry on, Bob.
